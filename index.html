About Us


Aaruush is the Annual National Level Techno-Management Fest of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. Since its inception in 2007, the motto of Aaruush has been "Rising in the spirit of Innovation", to keep striving to change the world with the power of innovation and give students a platform to showcase their skills on a national level. With an average annual footfall of over 75,000 and over 60 events, Aaruush has become one of the largest Techno-Management Fests in Asia, with colleges from all over India taking part in it. With an abundance of workshops, summits, interviews, expos, conferences, lectures, and the Aaruush Nites flowing with performances by artists from all around the world, Aaruush leaves a mark on all students alike, a memory to be revered for years to come!

Why become a Campus Ambassador?

The package is sealed with the cultivation of your knowledge and wisdom in coordinating and executing national-level events.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the Eligibility Criteria for a Campus Ambassador?

    All you need to become an official Campus Ambassador is to have is a valid College ID Card.

  • How do I apply to become a Campus Ambassador?

    You can click on the 'Apply Now' button above to get redirected to the registration portal.

  • Can there be multiple CAs from the same College?

    Yes, depending on the size of the college, Team Aaruush can appoint multiple CAs from the same campus.

  • How much time will I have to devote to the CA Program?

    As the official Campus Ambassador of Aaruush, you will need to invest two hours per week (on average) into doing the tasks that have been allotted to you. Your progress will be monitored every week and points will be awarded for the same.

Responsibilities


Publicity:

To publicize the events of Aaruush in and around your campus by being the face of the fest.



Representation:

To represent Aaruush where the fest's events are organized.



Social Media:

To promote the Aaruush social media handles for people to get appropriate information about the fest from the most reliable source.



Collaboration:

To communicate and collaborate with other colleges and organizations for their presence at Aaruush.



Visualization:

Visualizing and recommending ideas for the fest and administering them.



Co-action:

Collaborating with the team, along with maintaining a good reputation of the fest in your city.
